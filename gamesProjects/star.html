Star Sweepers

Description

Vacuum, sort, and recycle trash while skillfully dodging obstacles in an auto-scrolling cosmic environment. Communicate, strategize, and master the art of cosmic cleanup to keep the galaxy clutter-free and the stars shining bright! You may not be the Galactic Savior, but you'll still feel like a hero!

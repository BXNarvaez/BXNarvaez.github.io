Clock Out!!

Description

Fight your bosses (literally) as an unpaid intern in Clock Out!!, an arcade-style fighting game. Wreck chaos, climb the corporate ladder, and fight the system! Square up against unique bosses from different departments, from catty coworkers to slothful supervisors. Upgrade your resume and improve your skills as you climb up. Liquidate the company and bosses as you take down the higher-ups one by one!

What I learned

Clock Out!! is the first game I designed in a professional environment on a team of more than 4 people. Development was a true test for my skillset. I also learned to listen to each of my teammates. Some important lessons I took from this project:

  • Do not get married to any ideas pre-production. In the games industry, developers' first concern must be if their metaphor/idea for a game grabs an audience. Before it was a fighting game, Clock Out!! was an idle clicker, and after that it was an RPG. Also, the only animals were crows collecting artifacts in a desert. Many ideas that sounded good to the team but to no particular group of players had to be scrapped, and this was a very healthy practice that stung, but yielded much better results.
  • Identify the "first fun." What's the first thing the player will be doing when they pick up the game? It's likely the thing they'll be doing a lot of. This mechanic has to feel really fun to do. In this case the "first fun" is punching, and it was not as snappy as it is in the final version.
